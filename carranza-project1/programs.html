Our Programs

Our work focuses on prevention, cleanup, and restoration—so less trash reaches the ocean and marine habitats can recover.

What we do

Each program is designed to be hands-on, welcoming, and measurable. Join one event—or make it a monthly habit.

Coastal & River Cleanups

Volunteer events that remove trash before it reaches lakes, rivers, and oceans.

How to help

  • Bring gloves and a reusable water bottle
  • Invite a friend to join your team
  • Share cleanup photos to spread awareness

River-to-Ocean Prevention

Prevention projects that target waste “upstream” through smarter habits and community efforts.

How to help

  • Use reusables (bags, bottles, utensils)
  • Learn what your area actually recycles
  • Reduce single-use plastics at school/work

Habitat Restoration

Native planting and shoreline support to protect wildlife and improve water health.

How to help

  • Help plant native species during workdays
  • Support restoration supplies through donations
  • Join training sessions for volunteers

Wildlife Protection

Awareness and prevention to reduce harm to wildlife from fishing line, plastics, and litter.

How to help

  • Dispose of fishing line properly
  • Pick up small plastics while walking trails
  • Report hazardous waste areas to local teams

School Workshops

Interactive lessons that teach sustainability habits through real examples and activities.

How to help

  • Volunteer to help with activities
  • Share program resources with teachers
  • Start a student cleanup club

Community Advocacy

Simple advocacy actions that help communities reduce waste and improve recycling access.

How to help

  • Attend local cleanup planning meetings
  • Encourage refill stations and recycling bins
  • Share accurate sustainability information
