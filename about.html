About Blue Horizon Initiative

We're a community-focused nonprofit working to protect oceans by stopping pollution upstream, restoring habitats, and teaching sustainable habits that last.

Our mission

Our mission is to reduce ocean plastic and strengthen marine ecosystems through hands-on service, education, and partnerships. We believe the best conservation work is local, consistent, and shared.

Our values

  • Community-first action: We design programs that are welcoming and practical.
  • Science-informed choices: We partner with educators and conservation groups.
  • Transparency: We share goals, outcomes, and what we’re improving.
  • Long-term stewardship: Cleanups matter, but prevention matters even more.

Our story

  1. 2019 — Founded

    Started as a small group organizing weekend river cleanups.

  2. 2021 — Education partnerships

    Launched classroom workshops with local schools and youth groups.

  3. 2023 — Restoration projects

    Expanded into shoreline restoration and native habitat protection.

  4. 2025 — Regional collaboration

    Built partnerships with community organizations to scale prevention work.
