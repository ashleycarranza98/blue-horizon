How to volunteer

New volunteers are welcome—no experience needed.

  1. Pick an event

    Choose a cleanup, planting day, or workshop from our Programs page.

  2. Come prepared

    Wear closed-toe shoes and bring a reusable water bottle. We provide gloves and bags.

  3. Join the team

    Check in with the volunteer lead, get a quick safety briefing, and get started.

FAQ

Do I need experience to volunteer?

No. We’ll explain everything and provide supplies.

Are events family-friendly?

Most cleanups are. We recommend adult supervision for younger kids.

What should I bring?

Closed-toe shoes, sunscreen, and a reusable water bottle. We provide gloves and bags.
